Correlation Fragility

ML-Powered

Our XGBoost model analyses 24 market features (volatility, beta stability, correlation dynamics, volume shocks) to predict the probability of a correlation breakdown in the next 5 trading days.

0-30 STABLE — Correlation holding. Existing spread positions behave as expected. No action needed.
30-50 MODERATE — Early stress signs. We monitor more closely. Spread ideas for this pair stay active but we tighten watch.
50-70 ELEVATED — Relationship under pressure. New entries in related spreads use half position size. Stops widen automatically.
70-100 HIGH RISK — Correlation likely breaking. Stops on related spreads widen up to 1.4x. No new entries until score drops below 50.

These scores feed directly into our Telegram signals. When fragility rises, you'll see wider stops on P&L updates and a pause on new entries for affected spreads. When a HIGH RISK pair stabilises, it often creates the best entry opportunity — we alert that too.
