## Honest caveats

- **PIT regime tape uses today's frozen V3 CURATED-30 weights.** The labels are not contemporaneous walk-forward — they're "what would today's classifier have called this day". Acceptable for partitioning, but a contemporaneous walk-forward tape may shift cell membership and re-ranke the verdicts.
- **Bootstrap skipped this run** for speed. The provisional PASS for Reliance vs OMCs / EUPHORIA needs bootstrap validation (≥ 60% stability).
- **Mode A is fundamentally underpowered** at 24 months × news-trigger-rate × 5 regimes × 3 holds. Expanding the news history backwards is impossible (we never collected the headlines); we'd need 24 more months of forward news collection to revisit.
- **MaxDD failures are real signals**, not gate artifacts — the Defence baskets in RISK-ON earn high mean returns through positive-skew distributions with severe drawdown days. Sizing matters; equal-notional is wrong for these.
