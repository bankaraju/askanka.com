## 11. Single-touch holdout — DO NOT re-run

Per spec §10.4, this backtest is single-touch: the holdout slice is consumed by the first real run. If the verdict is FAIL or PARTIAL, do NOT re-run with adjusted parameters — that is a §10.4 violation and contaminates the next pass. Either:
- Accept the verdict and append to the registry (T11), or
- Pre-register a v2 hypothesis with new parameters and a fresh holdout window before re-running.

The runner enforces this via `holdout_touch_log.json`. If the file already exists with a different `run_id`, the runner aborts.
