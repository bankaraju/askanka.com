Portfolio vs Benchmarks

Methodology

Portfolio Construction

  • 15 stocks max across war-beneficiary sectors
  • Equal weight at inception (not market-cap weighted)
  • Pre-war baseline: Feb 27, 2026 closing prices
  • All returns converted to USD at live FX rates

Risk Management

  • 10% trailing stop from highest price since entry
  • Mechanical execution — no discretionary overrides
  • Re-entry requires both fundamental + technical confirmation
  • Replacement needs 2/3 score on our screening framework

Data Sources

  • Prices: EODHD API (primary) + yfinance (fallback)
  • Fundamentals: yfinance (analyst targets, P/E, EPS)
  • FX rates: Live via EODHD Forex
  • Technicals: Computed from 60-day price history

Update Schedule

  • Daily price capture: Mon-Sat 6:30 AM IST
  • Weekly aggregation: Saturday 9:00 AM IST
  • Portfolio tracker: runs with weekly aggregator
  • Report published: Saturday after market close

Limitations & Caveats

  • This is a paper portfolio — no actual trades are executed. Real-world returns would differ due to commissions, slippage, bid-ask spreads, and tax implications
  • Equal-weight construction does not reflect practical position sizing or risk budgeting
  • Trailing stops are calculated on closing prices only — intraday breaches are not captured
  • Analyst consensus targets were set at various dates and may not reflect current war dynamics
  • FX conversion uses daily close rates; actual conversion costs may vary
  • One-month track record is insufficient to validate any strategy; past performance is not indicative of future results
