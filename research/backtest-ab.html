What This Means for You

On 97% of trading days, the fragility model says "all clear" and you trade at full size with full confidence. On the rare 3% of days flagged as fragile, you still trade the same signals but with a smaller position and wider stops.

The net effect: better risk-adjusted returns (higher Sharpe) with shallower drawdowns. The fragility overlay doesn't try to predict the future — it just notices when the present is unusual.
