Why Correlations Matter

  • Spread trading relies on two baskets moving in opposite directions. You buy one sector, short another, and profit from the divergence.
  • When correlation breaks, your long and short legs start moving together — your hedge fails, and both sides lose.
  • Example: Defence vs IT had a 73% hit rate on Hormuz-related events. But if that correlation regime shifts, the spread stops working entirely.
